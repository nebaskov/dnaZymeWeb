About Us


Welcome to Sequence Craft, your go-to platform for predicting the catalytic properties of DNAzymes. Our user-friendly website offers access to a database of DNAzyme properties and tools for accurate prediction and visualization. With advanced algorithms, we simplify the estimation of catalytic properties, helping researchers and professionals optimize DNAzymes for various applications.

Sequence Craft simplifies the process of designing and analyzing DNAzymes, providing fast and reliable predictions based on sequence, buffer, and cofactor information. Updating the database and refining prediction models ensures high accuracy and usability for users in the field of nucleic acid catalysis.
