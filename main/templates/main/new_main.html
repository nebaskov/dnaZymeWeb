About Us
At SequenceCraft we are leading the way in creating innovative solutions for viral plant disease treatment. Our groundbreaking project focuses on the development of new catalytic DNA, known as DNAzymes. Using state-of-the-art machine learning methods, we aim to unlock the full potential of DNAzymes and revolutionize the way plant diseases are controlled. Our team of expert researchers are dedicated to harnessing the power of technology to engineer DNAzymes that will effectively target and neutralize viral infections in plants.
Redefining Crop Protection Against Viral Plant Diseases We are on a mission to develop a breakthrough in crop protection against viral plant diseases. With our research on DNAzymes, we are developing advanced defense mechanisms that will bolster plant health and secure sustainable food production for future generations
Machine Learning for Next-Generation DNAzyme Design Discover the future of oligonucleotides research as we combine machine learning, genetic algorithms, and gradient boosting models to revolutionize DNAzyme design. Experience the power of advanced techniques in creating highly efficient and tailored DNAzymes for sustainable agriculture.
